Explore the World with Coordinates

🌍 Coordinates are the backbone of geographic positioning, allowing us to pinpoint any location on Earth with precision. They consist of latitude and longitude, forming a universal language for navigation and exploration.

📏 Latitude and Longitude define a location’s position relative to the Equator and the Prime Meridian. Latitude measures north-south (0° to 90°), while longitude measures east-west (0° to 180°), guiding us across continents and oceans.

📡 GPS (Global Positioning System) harnesses satellite technology to deliver real-time coordinates. From smartphones to ships, GPS empowers us to navigate, track, and map our world with incredible accuracy.

🗺️ Google Maps transforms coordinates into interactive visuals. Whether plotting routes or zooming into street-level details, it brings the power of geospatial data to your fingertips, making the world accessible and understandable.
